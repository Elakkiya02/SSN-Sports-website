SSN Sports Infrastructure

You can achieve your full potential in sports here. Professional level facilities are available for a wide variety of indoor and outdoor games, supported by qualified coaches.

There are well equipped fitness centres for boys and girls. SSN Sports infrastructure is extensive and well-equipped, catering to a wide range of sports.

There is an indoor stadium as well as extensive space for outdoor sports. SSNCE sports facilities provide an opportunity for students to be physically active,interact with other students, and learn the importance of teamwork.

To keep students physically fit, the college has a gym on the main campus. All the gym equipment are modern catering to the physical needs of students.

Football

SSN has world class infrastructure for football.Football players at SSN often have a tremendous amount of aerobic capacity being able to go from walking to sprinting . Football is a great sport for burning fat because it works the muscles and your heart in different ways. Football builds more muscle mass and burns more fat by recruiting both slow-twitch and fast-twitch muscle fibers.Due to shifts between walking, running and sprinting, coordination is key to football. Body coordination is improved through the complex movements like dribbling, turning and passing, which are performed at varying rates of speed and direction. SSN is well equiped with professional level equipments and a ground for football.

ATHLETICS ( 400 Mts Track & Field)

Athletics is an important sport for students.Former college athletes say that have learned a lot of life lessons because of playing college athletics, but the most important benefits are teamwork, work ethic, and time management. Also, college athletes are put in situations where they have to learn and adapt or they won’t make it. SSN has a path breaking record when it comes to athlectics.SSN campus is fully equiped with the required ground facilities for athletics.
