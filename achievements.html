ACHIEVEMENTS


SSN has always set a benchmark when it comes to

achievements. At SSN ,students are driven

by a shared motivation to excel. We believe that our skill

in sports is represented through our numerous

achievements that have set milestones. SSNites are

persistent and thrive through all obstacles that pass by

their way. These achievements represent the grit of our

SSNites and the commitment we have towards fulfilling

our goal.


SSN SPORTS WINNERS' STORIES

Ravichandran Ashwin

International Cricket Player

Ravichandran Ashwin

My biggest takeaway from SSN was excellence.One of the biggest lessons for me was time management.It wasn't easy being on the ground,managing education.On top of it ,try and get through examinations and also mangaing my cricket career.I learnt excellence was a way of life and it was not just a word in the textbook or a word in the management class.For me SSN means
EXCELLENCE.

Anaka Alankamony

International Squash Player,
Arjuna Awardee(2010)

Anaka Alankamony

I believed squash guided my destiny. Thanks to the SSN College authorities, I was assured my sporting career would not lag behind. Having to travel four hours daily to and from college would have curtailed my training at the ISA,something I could ill afford. But my lecturers have given me the freedom to attend classes at my convenience and they also arrange special classes for me. I am very grateful to them.
