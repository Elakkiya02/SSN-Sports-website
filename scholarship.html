SCHOLARSHIPS

Scholarships

Why we do it?

At SSN, there is a perfect blend of sports and academics. Great importance is given to pursue excellence in sports and games at the College. The College has got infrastructure of International standards for developing growing talents and sports personalities. Our sports facilities are top-of-line among the ones in the professional institutions. The sports infrastructure comprises facilities for indoor and outdoor games. SSN doesn’t end its duty just at the encouragement level. We, as an institution, believe in nurturing the achievers and award them for their feats in different areas of expertise in the yearly scholarship programme. This is by far a unique programme in the country. It’s like an icing on the cake for the industrious achievers in sports and games.

Our approach

Our goal in the Office of Financial Aid is to help as many students as possible afford a quality education. We do this through a combination of need-based and merit-based scholarships. The average first-year grant and scholarship award from all sources is more than Rs.35,600, and the average total first-year financial aid package (including all grants and scholarships, federal student loans and campus employment) is more than Rs.40,000. We help students and families navigate the application process from start to finish.


Sports Quota

Every year we are giving 25 Engineering seats under the Management Sports quota. Students who have represented the National and International level tournaments for the following games and sports are eligible to apply for the sports quota admissions.

  • Athletics

  • Badminton

  • Basketball

  • Chess

  • Cricket

  • Football

  • Table Tennis

  • Tennis

  • Squash Racket
